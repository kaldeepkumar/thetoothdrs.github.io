Welcome to Tooth Doctor; an independent, Essex-based dental practice offering high quality dentistry. Established in 1992, the practice offers all aspects of private dental care for you and your family.

With clinic locations in Basildon and Thurrock, we offer an extensive range of dental services at the clinic, including preventative, emergency and cosmetic dental care.

Our dental practices are located in central areas and are proudly supported by an experienced team of some of the best dentists in Essex! Their combined experience offers patients access to specialist dental care in a warm and comfortable environment.
