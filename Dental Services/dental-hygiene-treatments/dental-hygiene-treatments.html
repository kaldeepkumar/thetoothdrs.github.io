Dental hygiene A healthy smile is a happy smile

Prevent dental problems by visiting our hygienists at regular intervals.

Our hygienist is friendly and fully qualified so you can rest assured that you will receive quality treatment at a competitive price to keep your mouth healthy and your smile beautiful.

We provide a comprehensive range of dental hygiene treatments that will help to prevent any permanent damage to your teeth and gums. So why not get in touch with us today to see how we can maintain your oral health.

Conditions that regular cleaning can help prevent

  • Cavities
  • Plaque Build Up
  • Gum Disease
  • Cardiovascular Disease (Heart attack and Stroke)
  • Bacterial Pneumonia
  • Low Birth Weight/Extreme High Birth Weight
  • Diabetes Complications

Clean & Polish Teeth

We provide a professional scale and polish. Your Tooth Doctor hygienist will carefully remove any build-up of plaque or hard deposits of calculus above and below the gum using manual or ultrasonic instruments.   

Avoid Gum Disease

Book regular visits to your Tooth Doctor hygienist. We maintain the health of your gums by regular thorough ultrasonic scaling and air polishing. 

Preventative Care

In addition to regular hygienist visits, ensure you brush twice a day, floss and avoid smoking.
