The Dental Implant Treatment in 5 Simple Steps

STEP 1

Free Dental Implant Consultation

We offer free dental implant consultations at both our Thurrock and Basildon dental practices.

During this appointment, one of our friendly dentists will take a look at your teeth and gums to determine whether dental implants are the right treatment for you.

STEP 2

Customised Treatment Plan

Next, we’ll take a Cone Beam CT (CBCT) scan to create a detailed 3D digital image of your mouth , which we’ll use to create a customised treatment plan for you.

Using this image, we’ll be able to evaluate the condition of your nerve pathways and jawbone and plan the best site for dental implants.

STEP 3

Professional Dental Cleaning

Before fitting your dental implants, we need to make sure your teeth and gums are as clean and healthy as possible.

We’ll carry out a prophylactic dental clean to remove any bacteria and plaque from your teeth and gums.

STEP 4

Fitting the Dental Implant 

Fitting your dental implant is a simple procedure. Your dentist will use local anaesthesia so you won’t be in any pain. They may also sedate you if you’re particularly nervous or you require extractions and bone augmentation.

With same-day implants, your dentist will fit both the metal frame and the false tooth or teeth in the same sitting.

STEP 5

Enjoy Your New Smile

With same-day implants, you’ll be ready to show off your smile as soon as the day after your implant procedure. Your dentist will advise you on how best to care for your implants post-treatment and which medication to take.

Simply stick to your post-treatment care routine and enjoy your stunning new teeth!

Dental Implants FAQs

What are dental implants?

Dental implants are metal frames that are inserted into your jawbone to replace your natural tooth root. The implants are made with metal that fuses with your bone (such as titanium — the most biocompatible metal). 

After the implant has fused with your jawbone, it is just as strong as a natural tooth root, so it can provide stable support for a replacement tooth or even multiple teeth. Crowns, dentures and bridges can all be mounted to implants.

How long does the treatment take?

Your Tooth Doctor dentist will assess your case during your free consultation. If the treatment is straightforward, it can be a little as eight to ten weeks from placement of the implant to fit of the final crown. However, sometimes treatment can take longer depending on the level of complexity of your case, with some patients not finishing for up to a year.
